The Current Situation and Trend Analysis of China’s Charitable Trust
Research Report on Social Impact Finance in China (No.2) 1 March 2024
Zhao Liu Yaqian Xiao Shiqi Lin

The overall report won China Social Enterprise and Impact Investing Forum’s For Good Research Award (2023)

ISBN: 9787522833538

Publisher: Social Sciences Academic Press (China)

Abstract: After the promulgation of the Trust Law of the People’s Republic of China, the Charity Law of the People’s Republic of China, and the Management Measures for Charitable Trusts, charitable trusts have become a new tool for coordinating various sectors of society to engage in philanthropic and charitable activities. After years of exploratory development, the number of charitable trusts and their asset scale has grown rapidly, with innovative designs in trust models and diverse categories of assets. However, the current situation of charitable trusts in China continues to confront challenges concerning insufficient support systems, ambiguous eligibility criteria, non-standardized information disclosure, and a requisite for enhanced societal awareness. Therefore, it is imperative to enhance policy and promotional support.

Decarbonizing Power Generation in Pennsylvania – A Roadmap Beyond RGGI
Student-Published Anthology of Research at Kleinman Magazine 1 Oct 2023
Zhao Liu

Pennsylvania’s heavy reliance on fossil fuels poses a significant challenge to the sustainable development of the power generation sector. Although Pennsylvania has joined the Regional Greenhouse Gas Initiative (RGGI) to reduce carbon emissions, ongoing legal disputes may compel Pennsylvania to withdraw from RGGI, resulting in significant environmental, health, and economic consequences. To mitigate these possible losses, remedial actions should tackle air pollution and environmental justice issues while introducing supplementary policies to RGGI to encourage rapid decarbonization.

The Current Situation and Trend Analysis of China’s Charitable Trust
Research Report on Social Impact Finance Blue Book (No.1) 1 December 2021
Zhao Liu

ISBN: 9787520192460

Publisher: Social Sciences Academic Press (China)

Abstract: The introduction of charitable trust system into China is relatively late. After The Trust Law of the People’s Republic of China, The Charity Law of the People’s Republic of China and The Management Measures of Charitable Trust were successively promulgated, the number of charitable trust filings in China has grown rapidly, with active different social entities’ participation. Charitable trust generally presents a steady and positive development trend and play an important role in promoting the third distribution and helping the development of public welfare. However, the laws and regulations and supporting systems for charitable trust still need to be further improved, and the public’s awareness of charitable trust also remains to be improved.

A-Share Listed Company Sustainable Development Value Assessment Report (2020) 1 January 2021
Wen Li Xinke Gu Zhao Liu Wenqi Hu

The overall report won China Soft Science Special Award (2021)

ISBN: 9787520176668

Publisher: Social Sciences Academic Press (China)

Abstract: After the founding of the People’s Republic of China, especially since the Reform and Opening-up, under the vigorous advocacy and promotion of the Chinese government, all sectors of society have joined poverty alleviation and it has achieved world-renowned achievements. In the process, Chinese enterprises, represented by large listed companies, have actively responded to the national call, taken the initiative to assume social responsibilities, and actively innovated in the process of poverty alleviation, making great contributions to winning the battle against poverty and promoting the achievement of many SDGs including ’no poverty’. This report takes A-share CSI 300 companies as the main research object, combines quantitative data analysis and innovative case analysis, and systematically studies the poverty alleviation actions of listed companies from various dimensions such as motivation, planning, input, methods, and outcomes.
